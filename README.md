# Kinora OS

> *One OS. Every platform. Every story.*

**Kinora OS** is not another streaming app. It is an operating system built specifically for your living room — a single intelligent layer that unifies every OTT platform, game library, music service, hardware device, and ticketing platform you own. Built for TVs. Built around you, not the platform.

---

## The Problem

- You pay for 4–5 OTT subscriptions and still can't find what you want to watch
- A movie you want is on a platform you don't subscribe to, available to rent, or leaving soon
- No single place to search across Netflix, Prime, Hotstar, Apple TV, ZEE5, SonyLIV simultaneously
- Smart subscription management doesn't exist — people overpay for apps they barely use
- Your PS5, Xbox, Steam Machine, and PC all need separate inputs, remotes, and wake-up routines
- Concert tickets are scalped, overpriced, or hidden behind gambling-style surge pricing
- OTT platforms push their own content over genuinely good content — biased algorithms
- No cross-media connections: a movie based on a game has no link to that game, anywhere

---

## The Vision

**Kinora OS is an operating system for your entire entertainment life.**

Think: the intelligence of Spotify Wrapped + the utility of JustWatch + the seamless hardware control of a smart home system — unified into a single TV OS with zero ads, zero manufacturer bloat, and zero friction.

Everything you own. Everything you subscribe to. One place.

---

## What It Does

### Unified Media Layer
Every piece of media you own or subscribe to — movies, shows, games, albums, concerts — lives in one collection, accessible without switching apps or inputs. Kinora OS is the single interface that talks to everything.

### Hardware Unification (HDMI-CEC)
Kinora OS runs on your TV and communicates directly with every peripheral plugged into it — PlayStation, Xbox, Steam Machine, PC. Where hardware supports it, Kinora OS uses HDMI-CEC to:

- Turn on your TV automatically when you launch Kinora OS
- Wake up your PlayStation or Xbox when you select a game — no controller needed
- Launch the game directly on the right device without changing inputs manually
- Manage all peripherals from one screen, one remote, one OS

### Software Unification
Steam OS, Spotify, Netflix, Prime Video, Hotstar, Apple TV, ZEE5, SonyLIV — every platform is integrated into a single OS that speaks to all of them simultaneously. One search bar. One algorithm. One home screen.

### Unified Search + Availability
- Search once → results across every connected platform
- Know instantly whether content is included in your subscription, rent/buy only, or leaving soon
- Get 'Leaving Soon' alerts so you never miss something before it disappears
- VPN suggestions for content not available in your region

### Smart Subscription Manager
- Link your OTT accounts and track expiry dates in one place
- Algorithm tells you which ONE platform to subscribe to this month based on what you actually want to watch
- Bundle deal surfacing — Jio, Airtel, BSNL offers, all in one view
- Rent/buy manager — all your purchased digital content, one shelf

### Music Concert Booking — No Scalpers
Concert tickets at fair, fixed prices. No surge pricing. No bots. No resellers marking up 10x.

- Tickets tied to verified ID — no anonymous bulk buying
- Gift transfers require recipient verification and expire within 5 days
- A clean, honest ticketing experience that works for fans, not scalpers

### The Vault (Coming Soon)
Deep-dive mode for every piece of content you love:
- Behind-the-scenes, interviews, and scene-level detail — better than Prime X-Ray
- Director filmographies, cinematographer credits, composer pages
- Cross-media linking: movie based on a game? The game is one click away. And vice versa
- Books, comics, and source material linked to their adaptations

### Full Platform Vision
- Console integration: select a game on Kinora OS → it launches on your PS5 or Xbox automatically
- Unified algorithm: recommendations across movies, shows, games, and music — one taste graph
- Digital album store: buy music the way it deserves to be bought
- Privacy-first TV OS: zero manufacturer ads, zero OTT bloat, fully yours
